Aeronautical Engineers Association of Kenya

Registration Requirements

To be registered as a member of this association bringing together aviation professionals, you must present:

  1. Bachelor's degree or Diploma certificate from a recognized institution
  2. Original KCSE certificate
  3. Valid Personal accident insurance cover
  4. Copy of National ID
  5. KCAA registration number
  6. A minimum of 5 years experience in a busy aviation environment
  7. Endorsement letter from current employer
  8. Next of Kin contact details
  9. At least two referees
